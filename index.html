Risu4DB a Visual SQL Query Builder

Risu4DB is a database management application that allows you to connect to MySQL databases from your mobile. With its simple interfaces that allow you to create SQL queries in a visual way, you can create, manage and maintain your databases quickly and easily.

how it works

1

download the app

For Android download and install the app, on Windows unzip the .zip file.

2

add a new connection

Indicate the ip address or host name, port number, username and password.

3

Select a connection

Swipe left on the connection of your choice and click the "connect" button.

4

choose a database

Swipe left on the database of your choice and click the "manage" button.
